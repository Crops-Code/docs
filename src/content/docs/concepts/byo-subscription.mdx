You don't have to run on ClusterCode's platform AI credits. **Bring your own AI
credentials** — either an **API key** or your **AI subscription** (your Claude or
Codex OAuth login) — and your [Runs](/concepts/runs),
[Schedules](/concepts/schedules), and [multi-agent
orchestration](/guides/multi-agent-runs) will draw on **your** account.

## Connecting your credentials

Add your credentials in **Settings**:

- An **API key** (e.g. an Anthropic or OpenAI key), or
- Your **AI subscription** — the OAuth login for an official CLI (Claude Code,
  Codex).

Once connected, [Nova](/concepts/nova) uses them automatically when it runs your
jobs.

## How it's used — the credential cascade

When a job needs AI, ClusterCode resolves credentials in a defined order:

1. **Your connected subscription / OAuth login** is preferred first.
2. **Your connected API key** is used next.
3. **Platform AI credits** are the fallback when you haven't connected anything.

So your own subscription or key is spent **instead of (or before)** the platform
[AI credits](/guides/ai-credits) — the same cascade the
[Nova / AI-credits note](/guides/ai-credits) describes. This lets you put your
existing AI plan to work and keep your platform credits for everything else.

## How your credentials are stored today

Credentials you connect are stored **per user** and **encrypted at rest**
(AES-256-GCM). They are **never shared across users** — your key or subscription
authenticates only your own jobs. This applies to both Cloud workers and your own
[BYOM workers](/concepts/workers).

<Aside type="note" title="Coming soon — fully local, zero-storage passthrough">
  We're building a path where, on your **own BYOM worker**, your existing **local
  CLI login** will be used directly — so the token will **never leave your machine
  and will never be stored in our database** at all. That fully zero-storage
  local-passthrough model is a **planned** capability, not yet available. Until it
  ships, connected credentials are stored encrypted at rest as described above.
</Aside>
