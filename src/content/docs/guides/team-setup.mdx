ClusterCode supports multiple tenants, organizations, and team members. This guide covers inviting members and managing access.

## Concepts

- **Organization** — your company or team umbrella
- **Tenant** — a workspace within an organization (e.g. per-project or per-team)
- **Member** — a user with access to one or more tenants
- **Seat** — a billable slot for a member

## Inviting Members

From the Admin Portal:

1. Navigate to **Users → Members**
2. Click **Invite**
3. Enter the email address
4. Assign a role (Admin or Member)
5. Select which tenants they can access

The invitee receives an email and joins on their next sign-in.

## Roles

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **Admin** | Manage members, tenants, billing, all worker/container operations |
| **Member** | Launch containers, view workers, access assigned tenants |

## Limits by Plan

| Plan | Seats | Tenants | Organizations |
|------|-------|---------|---------------|
| Starter | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| Pro | 1 included (+$20/seat, up to 50) | 5 | 1 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Unlimited | 10 |

## Admin Portal

Team management happens in the Admin Portal at `admin.clustercode.io`:

- Manage members and invitations
- Configure tenants
- View usage and billing
- Set notification preferences
