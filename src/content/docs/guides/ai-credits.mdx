---
title: AI Credits
description: Understand AI credit usage, BYOK, and budget management.
---

CropsCode includes AI credits for CropPilot sessions and container agents. You can use the platform allowance or bring your own API keys.

## Platform Allowance

Each plan includes a monthly AI credit allowance:

| Plan | AI Allowance | Top-ups |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Starter | $2/mo | Not available |
| Pro | $6/mo | Available |
| Enterprise | $10/user/mo | Available |

Credits are consumed by:
- CropPilot conversations in the dashboard
- AI agent activity inside containers (when using platform keys)

## Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can provide your own API keys. This means:
- No platform credit consumption
- Use your own rate limits and pricing
- Full control over provider choice

Configure BYOK credentials in the orchestrator settings. See [Environment Variables](/reference/environment-variables) for the specific keys.

## Budget Management

The orchestrator dashboard shows:
- Current session budget usage (percentage)
- Monthly allowance remaining
- Per-session cost breakdown

When approaching your budget limit, CropPilot displays a warning banner suggesting you add your own API key or upgrade your plan.

## Live Sessions

Live sessions are interactive CropPilot conversations:

| Plan | Sessions per Container | Total Sessions |
|------|----------------------|----------------|
| Starter | 3 | 3 |
| Pro | 3 | 15 |
| Enterprise | 3 | Unlimited |
