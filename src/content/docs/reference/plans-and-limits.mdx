## Plans

| | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $0/mo | $9.99/mo | $29.99/mo | $99/first user |
| **Additional seats** | — | — | $20/seat (up to 50) | Degressive pricing |

**Free** is a real plan tier, not a trial. It runs on your **own machine (BYOM)**
using your **own Claude key (BYOK)** — there's no platform AI or compute
allowance, no cloud/greenhouse containers, and no schedules. It's the simplest
way to self-host CropsCode; upgrade to Starter+ for platform AI, scheduling, and
managed compute.

## Resource Limits

| Resource | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|----------|------|---------|-----|-----------|
| Self-hosted workers | 1 | 2 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Concurrent containers | 3 | 5 | 15 | Unlimited |
| Custom images | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Concurrent runs | 1 | 1 | 5 | 10 |
| Cron schedules | 0 | 1 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Live sessions per container | 1 | 3 | 3 | 3 |
| Total live sessions | 1 | 3 | 15 | Unlimited |
| Container history | 1 day | 14 days | 90 days | 365 days |
| Tenants | 1 | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| Organizations | 1 | 1 | 1 | 10 |
| Seats | 1 | 1 | 50 | Unlimited |

On a capped plan, only the **newest N custom images** stay launchable (Free keeps
the newest 3); older ones are locked until you upgrade or remove some.

## AI & Compute Allowances

| | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| AI allowance | — | $2/mo | $6/mo | $10/user/mo |
| Compute allowance | — | 10 hrs/mo | 50 hrs/mo | 200 hrs/mo |
| Top-ups | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) | Required | No | Yes | Yes |

On **Free**, AI runs only through your own key (BYOK) — there's no platform AI
pool, so runs and CropPilot require an Anthropic credential configured under
**Settings → Secrets**.
